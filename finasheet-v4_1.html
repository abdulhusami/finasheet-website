FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Still unsure? Chat with an expert on WhatsApp, we answer in minutes, not days.

Is the Starter plan really free forever?+

Yes. Starter is free forever, no trial, no time limit. It's built for freelancers and very small businesses who need clean basic accounting without paying for features they don't use.

What happens after June end for launch users?+

Early users get full premium access free until June end. After that, you can continue on a paid plan, drop down to Starter (which is free forever), or keep exploring without any pressure. Your data stays with you either way.

Is setup really included?+

Yes. We set up your chart of accounts, tax codes, and opening balances, import your existing data where possible, and align your corporate tax setup with FTA guidelines. Most accounts are fully ready within 24 hours of signup.

Is Finasheet really built for UAE businesses?+

Yes, this is the core of the product. VAT, corporate tax, e-invoicing readiness, and FTA-aligned setup logic are built in from day one, not added as patches on a global template. The founder has 10+ years of hands-on UAE accounting experience.

Can I import from Zoho, Tally, QuickBooks, or Excel?+

In most cases, yes. Our team handles data imports during setup, contacts, opening balances, chart of accounts, and historical transactions where feasible. Share what you have and we'll guide the migration. It's included free with every paid plan.

Does it handle VAT and corporate tax correctly?+

Yes. Standard 5% VAT, zero-rated, and exempt flows are all handled. Corporate tax reporting is aligned with FTA guidelines. Quarterly VAT returns and annual corporate tax prep are built into the reporting suite.

Is it suitable for non-accountants?+

Yes. Owner-facing dashboards are clean and plain-language, you don't need accounting training to read your revenue, profit, and tax due. For deeper bookkeeping, you can bring in an accountant (yours or ours) with role-based access.

Can my accountant use Finasheet too?+

Absolutely. You can invite your accountant with a dedicated role and granular permissions. They get accountant-speed workflows, shortcuts, voucher numbering, and period locking, the tools they actually expect.
